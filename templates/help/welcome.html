This application provides a detailed and easy-to-use map based on data from OpenStreetMap project, check out www.openstreetmap.org for more information.

The data shown in the application might not have the same level of accuracy for all locations in the map. If you find inaccuracies and problems with the map or points of interest and want to contribute, you can visit www.openstreetmap.org and edit the map to update it.
The application will be regurarly updated to reflect the data from the OpenStreetMap database, but as we can't guarantee the perfect accuracy of the information, the application is not intended be used as a navigator.

You can navigate the map, show, search and browser points of interest with a very simple touch-based interface.

Use the buttons in the bottom of this popup to navigate help tips.

You can close the help at any time by clicking on the 'x' near the title of the tips and reopen it by clicking on the icon at the upper-right corner of your screen.
